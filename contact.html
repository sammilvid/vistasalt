ABOUT US

Our Story

VISTA SALT was born from a passion for adventure and the stunning landscapes around Salt Lake City. As outdoor enthusiasts who spend countless hours hiking, cycling, and exploring Utah's beautiful terrain, we understand the critical importance of proper hydration and electrolyte balance.

What makes our story unique is our deep connection to the outdoor community and our commitment to providing products that actually work. We're not just another supplement company—we're athletes ourselves who demand the highest quality from everything we use.

Our scientifically balanced formula isn't just another sports drink—it's a carefully crafted blend designed for serious athletes and outdoor adventurers who demand the best. With zero sugar, clean ingredients, and optimal mineral ratios, VISTA SALT helps you perform at your peak when it matters most.

Our Mission

To make premium, scientifically-balanced electrolyte solutions accessible and affordable for everyone—because proper hydration shouldn't be a luxury.

Our Values

Clean ingredients, scientific precision, honest performance, and supporting the outdoor community.
